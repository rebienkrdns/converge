# Glossary

- **Council**: the group of specialists responsible for evaluating a decision.
- **Permanent Council**: the baseline set of specialists present in every review.
- **Adaptive Council**: domain specialists activated when the problem requires them.
- **Engine**: a conceptual component that transforms inputs into a structured output.
- **Gate**: a blocking checkpoint that can stop implementation.
- **Decision Packet**: the bundle of context, evidence, options, risks, and recommended action.
- **Confidence**: the measured strength of the decision, based on evidence quality and disagreement level.
- **Evidence**: verifiable material used to support or reject a claim.
- **Assumption**: a statement accepted temporarily because evidence is incomplete.
- **Risk**: a credible adverse outcome with meaningful impact and non-trivial likelihood.
- **Pack**: a domain-specific extension that adapts the framework for a technology or operating context.
