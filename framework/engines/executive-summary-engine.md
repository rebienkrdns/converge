# Executive Summary Engine

## Purpose

Compress the full evaluation into a concise decision summary for leadership and implementers.

## Inputs

- Recommendation.
- Matrix outputs.
- Gate status.
- Top risks and assumptions.

## Outputs

- Executive summary.
- Decision status.
- Key tradeoffs.
- Next action.

## Conceptual Algorithm

Retain only the decision essentials: what is being decided, why, what blocks it, and what must happen next.

## Responsibilities

- Preserve fidelity while reducing volume.
- Emphasize actionable clarity.
- Avoid oversimplification.

## Interaction

Consumes outputs from all major engines and produces the final human-readable decision record.
