# Impact Engine

## Purpose

Estimate the operational, product, and organizational consequences of the decision.

## Inputs

- User impact.
- Technical blast radius.
- Delivery cost.
- Operational burden.

## Outputs

- Impact rating.
- Positive and negative consequence summary.
- Scope of affected systems or teams.

## Conceptual Algorithm

Model impact across time horizons and stakeholders, then surface the most material consequences rather than a generic severity label.

## Responsibilities

- Quantify where possible.
- Explicitly describe second-order effects.
- Prevent hidden cost from being overlooked.

## Interaction

Supports the Decision Matrix Engine and the Gate Engine.
