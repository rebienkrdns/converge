# Decision Matrix Engine

## Purpose

Translate the decision packet into a structured evaluation across canonical dimensions.

## Inputs

- Quality criteria.
- Evidence quality.
- Risk and impact analysis.
- Recommended option set.

## Outputs

- Quality Score.
- Confidence.
- Evidence rating.
- Impact rating.
- Recommendation.
- Executive Summary.

## Conceptual Algorithm

Score each dimension only from explicit criteria, then derive the recommendation from the combined shape of quality, confidence, evidence sufficiency, and impact.

## Responsibilities

- Make judgment explicit.
- Prevent arbitrary scoring.
- Keep ratings tied to visible evidence.

## Interaction

Feeds the Gate Engine, Recommendation Engine, and Executive Summary Engine.
