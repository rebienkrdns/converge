# Evidence Engine

## Purpose

Collect, classify, and normalize evidence used in a decision.

## Inputs

- Code and architecture artifacts.
- Metrics and logs.
- Benchmarks and experiments.
- Domain expert input.
- Prior incidents or history.

## Outputs

- Evidence inventory.
- Evidence quality classification.
- Gaps and contradictions.

## Conceptual Algorithm

Tag each item by relevance, recency, reliability, and directness. Reject unsupported assertions and separate facts from inferences.

## Responsibilities

- Preserve provenance.
- Distinguish evidence from opinion.
- Expose missing data.

## Interaction

Feeds the Confidence Engine, Impact Engine, and Decision Matrix Engine.
