# Convergence Engine

## Purpose

Reduce many observations, opinions, and candidate approaches into a coherent decision shape.

## Inputs

- Decision packet.
- Specialist evaluations.
- Evidence inventory.

## Outputs

- Shortlist of viable options.
- Dominant tradeoffs.
- Preferred direction or unresolved conflict.

## Conceptual Algorithm

Group evidence by concern, remove duplicate arguments, surface contradictions, and identify the smallest option set that still covers the problem.

## Responsibilities

- Synthesize debate into convergence.
- Preserve important disagreement.
- Avoid premature closure.

## Interaction

Consumes outputs from the Evidence Engine, Decision Matrix Engine, and Critique system.
