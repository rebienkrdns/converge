# Confidence Engine

## Purpose

Measure how strongly the framework believes the conclusion is supportable.

## Inputs

- Evidence strength.
- Degree of specialist agreement.
- Completeness of the decision packet.

## Outputs

- Confidence level.
- Confidence rationale.
- Confidence risks.

## Conceptual Algorithm

Confidence rises when evidence is strong, assumptions are few, and disagreement is resolved or explicitly bounded.

## Responsibilities

- Separate conviction from proof.
- Penalize hidden uncertainty.
- Highlight what would lower confidence.

## Interaction

Supports the Decision Matrix Engine and the Self Critique system.
