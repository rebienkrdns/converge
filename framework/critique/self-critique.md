# Self Critique

Self critique is the built-in adversarial layer of Converge.

It must be applied after the initial convergence and before final delivery.

## Assumptions

List every assumption that carries decision weight. Separate load-bearing assumptions from low-impact conveniences.

## Blind Spots

Identify what the council may not be seeing because of available data, team perspective, or domain limits.

## Missing Evidence

State which facts are still unknown and whether the decision can proceed safely without them.

## Counterarguments

Present the strongest opposing view, not a weak strawman.

## Confidence Analysis

Explain why confidence is high or low, and what would move it materially.

## Future Risks

Describe risks that may not matter immediately but could invalidate the decision later.

## Character

This is a reasoning discipline, not a checklist. Each section should change the quality of the decision, not merely record it.
