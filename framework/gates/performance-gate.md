# Performance Gate

## Purpose

Block decisions that cannot meet the expected performance profile.

## Approval Conditions

- Load assumptions are stated.
- Bottlenecks are understood.
- Headroom is acceptable.

## Blocking Conditions

- No meaningful performance evidence.
- Avoidable critical-path amplification.
- Unbounded resource growth.

## Evidence Required

- Benchmark or load model.
- Critical-path analysis.
- Resource profile.
