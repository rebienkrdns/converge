# Observability Gate

## Purpose

Block work that cannot be meaningfully observed in production.

## Approval Conditions

- Key events are observable.
- Errors can be detected and correlated.
- Recovery paths can be measured.

## Blocking Conditions

- No monitoring or tracing plan.
- Failure states are invisible.
- Operational ownership is undefined.

## Evidence Required

- Metrics, logs, traces, or dashboards.
- Alerting and ownership plan.
- Recovery verification approach.
