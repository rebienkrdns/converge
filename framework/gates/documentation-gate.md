# Documentation Gate

## Purpose

Block delivery when the decision cannot be understood or operated from the available documentation.

## Approval Conditions

- The decision is documented clearly.
- Operational behavior is described.
- Future maintainers can reconstruct the rationale.

## Blocking Conditions

- Missing decision record.
- Unclear operational instructions.
- Hidden assumptions.

## Evidence Required

- Decision record.
- Operational notes.
- Glossary-aligned terminology.
