# Architecture Gate

## Purpose

Block designs that do not have a coherent system shape or sustainable boundary model.

## Approval Conditions

- Responsibilities are separated clearly.
- Dependencies are directional and explainable.
- The design matches the expected lifespan and scale.

## Blocking Conditions

- Architecture is justified only by habit or preference.
- Coupling is unresolved.
- The decomposition increases confusion more than clarity.

## Evidence Required

- Architecture diagram or narrative.
- Boundary rationale.
- Tradeoff analysis.
