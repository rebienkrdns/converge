# Maintainability Gate

## Purpose

Block implementations whose long-term support cost is unacceptably high.

## Approval Conditions

- Code and design remain legible.
- Dependencies are manageable.
- Future change paths are plausible.

## Blocking Conditions

- Excessive incidental complexity.
- Hard-to-change abstractions.
- Unclear ownership or lifecycle.

## Evidence Required

- Maintainability rationale.
- Change impact analysis.
- Ownership and dependency map.
