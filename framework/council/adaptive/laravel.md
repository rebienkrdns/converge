# Laravel Specialist

## Purpose

Evaluate decisions within Laravel applications, ecosystems, and deployment patterns.

## Focus

- Service container usage.
- Eloquent data access patterns.
- Queue, event, and middleware design.

## Special Questions

- Is framework convention being respected or fought unnecessarily?
- Are ORM choices creating hidden performance or coupling risks?

## Interaction

Extends the permanent council with framework-specific maintainability and performance review.
