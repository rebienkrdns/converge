# Adaptive Council

The adaptive council extends the permanent council with specialists relevant to the domain under review.

It is activated when the problem contains language, risk, infrastructure, or regulations that the permanent council cannot fully adjudicate alone.

## Activation Rule

Add domain specialists when at least one of the following is true:

- The stack has strong domain-specific constraints.
- The system interacts with regulated data or external systems.
- The implementation relies on an operational platform with non-trivial semantics.
- The decision requires specialist judgment not covered by the permanent council.

## Extension Rule

Adaptive specialists extend the permanent council rather than replace it. They add domain depth; they do not redefine the meaning of approval, rejection, confidence, or evidence.
