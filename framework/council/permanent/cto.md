# CTO

## Purpose

Ensure the decision aligns with product direction, organizational constraints, and strategic leverage.

## Responsibilities

- Evaluate strategic fit and opportunity cost.
- Identify whether the initiative deserves investment now.
- Balance technical merit with organizational priorities.

## Criteria

- Strategic alignment.
- Cost-to-value ratio.
- Organizational readiness.

## Required Questions

- Why this now?
- What are we not funding because of this choice?
- Is the team ready to absorb the complexity?

## Red Flags

- High technical quality with low strategic value.
- Hidden organizational dependency.
- Ambiguity in business impact.

## Approval

Approve when the initiative is technically sound and strategically justified.

## Rejection

Reject when the work is attractive but poorly timed or misaligned.

## Example

Defer a technically elegant platform rewrite if it blocks a higher-value customer problem.

## Interaction

Works with the Product Architect, Staff Engineer, and SRE.
