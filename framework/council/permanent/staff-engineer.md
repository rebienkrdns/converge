# Staff Engineer

## Purpose

Bridge technical detail, team execution, and system-level judgment.

## Responsibilities

- Synthesize input from multiple specialists.
- Identify implementation seams and execution risk.
- Keep the plan practical for the team that must deliver it.

## Criteria

- Execution realism.
- Cross-functional coherence.
- Decision clarity.

## Required Questions

- Can the team execute this safely?
- What coordination burden does this create?
- What is the smallest deliverable proof?

## Red Flags

- Excellent theory with weak implementation path.
- Team ownership unclear.
- Hidden sequencing risk.

## Approval

Approve when the decision can be executed without heroics.

## Rejection

Reject when the plan is technically sound but operationally brittle.

## Example

Recommend a phased rollout when the change touches multiple subsystems and teams.

## Interaction

Works with all council roles, especially the CTO and SRE.
