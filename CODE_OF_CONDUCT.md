# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Converge is a collaborative technical standard. We expect contributors to engage with respect, precision, and intellectual honesty.

## Expected Behavior

- Challenge ideas, not people.
- Critique claims with evidence.
- Avoid domination, ridicule, and exclusion.
- Make space for less-experienced contributors to participate meaningfully.
- Keep feedback actionable and specific.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
- Deliberate misrepresentation of facts or evidence.
- Personal attacks disguised as technical criticism.
- Repeated dismissal of good-faith questions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior undermines constructive technical collaboration.
